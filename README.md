# VentureBeat (venturebeat)

VentureBeat (https://venturebeat.com/) is a tech media site covering AI, gaming, and enterprise innovation. Founded in 2006 by Matt Marshall, VentureBeat is headquartered in San Francisco and serves over 12 million monthly page views. The publication focuses on transformative technology coverage for business and enterprise decision-makers, with particular depth in artificial intelligence, machine learning, gaming, and enterprise software. VentureBeat offers newsletters including the AI Weekly and AI Beat, editorial events, membership plans, and a broad RSS feed infrastructure for syndicated content distribution.
