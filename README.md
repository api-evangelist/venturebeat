# VentureBeat

VentureBeat is a leading technology news and media company covering artificial intelligence, enterprise technology, gaming, and the business of innovation. Founded in 2006 by Matt Marshall and based in San Francisco, VentureBeat serves over 12 million monthly page views. The publication focuses on transformative technology coverage for business and enterprise decision-makers, with particular depth in AI, machine learning, cybersecurity, and enterprise software.

VentureBeat does not offer a public developer REST API. Content is accessible via RSS 2.0 feeds by category.
