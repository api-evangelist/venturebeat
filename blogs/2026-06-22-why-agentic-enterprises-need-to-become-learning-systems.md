Organizations accumulate valuable knowledge daily through AI corrections and incident resolutions, yet rarely convert this into reusable institutional knowledge. The article (presented by Splunk) argues that competitive advantage will belong to enterprises building systems that let AI improve over time in a controlled, trustworthy way.
